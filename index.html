About DJ Taye

Music Has Always Been The Thread

Music has always been part of my story. Growing up in Georgia, I was inspired by my father, who introduced me to the world of DJing and helped spark a passion that still drives me today.

I launched Flatlyne Entertainment during COVID and spent countless hours learning, practicing, and building something I could be proud of.

My goal is simple: make the event feel like you. The music, the energy, and the flow should match your crowd, your vision, and the memories you want people to leave with.
